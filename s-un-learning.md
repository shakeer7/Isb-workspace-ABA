Here’s the segregation:

Supervised Learning (learns from labeled data)



|Model              |Use Case                             |
|-------------------|-------------------------------------|
|Linear Regression  |House price prediction               |
|Logistic Regression|Spam detection, loan approval        |
|Decision Tree      |Insurance approval                   |
|Random Forest      |Fraud detection                      |
|SVM                |Face recognition, text classification|
|Naive Bayes        |Spam filtering, sentiment analysis   |
|Neural Network     |Voice assistants, recommendations    |
|CNN                |Face unlock, medical scan analysis   |
|RNN                |Text prediction, subtitles           |
|LSTM               |Stock prediction, speech             |
|Transformer        |Translation, AI chatbots             |
|LLM                |Chatbots, coding assistants          |
|XGBoost            |Fraud detection, ranking             |

Unsupervised Learning (finds patterns without labels)



|Model                  |Use Case                            |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------|
|K-Means Clustering     |Customer segmentation               |
|Hierarchical Clustering|DNA analysis, document grouping     |
|Autoencoders           |Anomaly detection, image compression|
|GANs                   |Deepfakes, AI art generation        |
|Diffusion Models       |AI image creation                   |

Neither / Special Category



|Model                 |Category                            |
|----------------------|------------------------------------|
|Reinforcement Learning|Reward-based learning (own category)|
|Recommendation System |Hybrid (supervised + unsupervised)  |
|Time Series Models    |Can be either                       |
|Ensemble Models       |Meta-technique, not a category      |
